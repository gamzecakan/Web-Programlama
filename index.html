Salsa Dance


Salsa dance is a partner dance of Latin American origin. This dance is known for its fast rhythm and passionate movements.


Tango Dance


Tango is a type of dance that originated in Argentina. Tango, an emotional and dramatic dance, is known for its dancers who often hug each other tightly.


Swing Dance


Swing dance is an American dance that was popular from the 1920s to the 1940s. It is an energetic and joyful type of dance accompanied by live music.


Waltz Dance


Waltz dance is a dance type of Austrian origin that emerged in the 18th century. It is a classical dance type that alternates with graceful and flowing steps.


Rumba Dance


Rumba dance is a Latin dance known for its warm, emotional and rhythmic steps, especially of Cuban origin.

Twist Dance


Twist dance is a dance genre known for its energetic and rotary steps that became popular in the United States in the early 1960s.
